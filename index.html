About Our Company

Zinc Corp is an IT company based in Yogyakarta (Indonesia) founded in 2012. Our services including IT Infrastructure, IT Consultant, and System Analyst. Our main priority are delivering the best quality of services to all of our customer and satisfy their need in technologies problem.

Our mission is to help the consument to achieve not only optimal productivity but also capabilites to handle more challenges that requires technology to work. With our services the consument can focus only to work from their side and we will take care the rest of their work that requires technologies to work with.

Our Services

IT Infrastructures

Responsible for the implementation and operations of all technology infrastructures which includes data center, network and server services, telephony, service monitoring, user support/help desk, workstation management, servers, storage and related software

IT Consultant

External analysts who evaluate a company's IT systems to help them meet their business objectives. Their primary duties include analyzing and diagnosing a company's IT infrastructure, understanding a client's business needs, and designing and implementing a technology solution

System Analyst

Analyze how well software, hardware and the wider IT system fit the business needs of their employer or of a client. They write requirements for new systems and may also help implement them and monitor their effectiveness
